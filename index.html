⚙ RPG — Choose Your Core

Each snake evolves their core over time — eating apples, killing enemies, or triggering the ability all grant XP. Early tiers unlock faster than later ones. Use your action key in-game to activate the ability.

★ Side Buffs — Pick 2 per player

Each player chooses 2 passive buffs. Buffs stack with cores and apply for the whole match.
